Volunteerism is an important and increasingly popular mechanism for young people to bring about constructive changes in society. It has been highlighted that promoting volunteerism by rewarding and recognizing a volunteer’s efforts towards creating a conducive environment is looked upon as one of the crucial elements towards encouraging people to be a social volunteer.

Kites Foundation announces Kalam Awards 2021 to acknowledge and appreciate the journey of young individuals and communities who have volunteered and contributed positively to bring about a constructive change in the surrounding society and led a step forward to ensure a better state of living from the developmental aspect.

Category of Awards

Award for the Best Volunteer

The Best Volunteer Award is presented to recognise exemplary voluntary contributions by young individuals in the service sector. It aims to encourage youth to engage in meaningful voluntary activities. This award reflects the exceptional array of individuals working in our nation's vibrant charity and non-profit sectors.

Award for the Best NGO/Community

The Best NGO/Community Award is presented to the outstanding functionaries to recognise their unwavering voluntary contributions to the society. The award is to celebrate the efforts of the non-government non-profit organizations or communities and to reinforce their practices in our society. The award also encourages greater participation from the young individuals to work in their community and beyond, for the nation's development.

Award for the Best Project

The Best Project Award hunts impactful projects that inspire a whole new way of bringing about a ‘change’. The award will recognize those wonderful projects that were rooted from an honest purpose to bring about sustainable development.

  • Am I a Volunteer?

    Every day, millions of individuals across the planet engage in development initiatives through volunteer actions – both large and small – in order to improve conditions for others and for themselves, their families and communities. If you have been working towards bringing change in your society and have volunteered in any of the four categories, then you are a volunteer

    • Institutions/Colleges (NSS,NYKS,CSOs)
    • NGOs
    • Community (Working directly with a certain community.)
    • Social entrepreneurs
