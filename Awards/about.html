ABOUT US ABOUT

Kites Foundation announces Kalam Awards 2021 to acknowledge and appreciate the journey of young individuals and communities who have volunteered and contributed positively to bring about a constructive change in the surrounding society and led a step forward to ensure a better state of living from the developmental aspect.

Award for the Best Volunteer

The Best Volunteer Award is presented to recognise exemplary voluntary contributions by young individuals in the service sector. It aims to encourage youth to engage in meaningful voluntary activities. This award reflects the exceptional array of individuals working in our nation's vibrant charity and non-profit sectors.

Eligibility Criteria for the Best Volunteer Award
  • Indian citizens till 30 years of age.
  • Minimum six months of volunteering experience, by either working or running an NGO, or contributing to society positively in your unique ways.
Award for the Best NGO/Community

The Best NGO/Community Award is presented to the outstanding functionaries to recognise their unwavering voluntary contributions to the society. The award is to celebrate the efforts of the non-government non-profit organizations or communities and to reinforce their practices in our society. The award also encourages greater participation from the young individuals to work in their community and beyond, for the nation's development.

Eligibility Criteria for the Best NGO/Community Award
  • Founded or/and currently running with Indian citizens below the age of 40 as Board members.
  • If an NGO, it must be registered under any of these:
    • Society Registration Act
    • Company Registration Act
    • Nehru Youth Kendra Sangathan (NYKS)
  • If a community, it must contain ………………...
  • Minimum 2 years of active working history in the recent past.
  • Organization or the community must emphasise on youth-led developments and sustainable practices.
  • Provide aid for the well-being of under-priveliged communities and build support mechanisms for their upliftment.
Award for the Best Project

The Best Project Award hunts impactful projects that inspire a whole new way of bringing about a ‘change’. The award will recognize those wonderful projects that were rooted from an honest purpose to bring about sustainable development.

Eligibility Criteria for the Best Project Award
  • Initiative by an NGO registered in India or headed by an Indian citizen.
  • Project must be at least 60% completed.
  • Beneficiary of the project must be any Indian community, as a whole or a section of it, rather than a specific individual
  • Project should meet minimum three Sustainable Development Goals (SDG).
  • Uniqueness and innovation of the idea, primary purpose and immediate beneficiaries of the project, scale of success in terms of development in the community etc will be evaluated.

How to Apply?

  • Applications can be submitted through the online portal available on the website.
  • Applications can be made by themselves or in cases, nominations and recommendations by a second party are also acceptable. In the case of projects, one NGO can submit a maximum of 3 projects only.
  • Selected entries for the second phase will have to submit a detailed report of their activity, in the prescribed format. In cases, nominated or recommended entries get selected to the second phase, the nominee is solely responsible to proceed with and not the applicant. In no cases, KITES Foundation will be responsible, if the nominee refuses to proceed further and the entry will stand cancelled, thereafter.
  • Entries selected for the third and final phase will have to report before an interview panel in person (for volunteer award), with all or minimum 3 board members (for NGO/community award), with all or minimum 3 project coordinators (for project award)

When to apply?

  • Applications will be open from August 1 2021 to August 20 2021.
  • Detailed report submission for the selected, will be from August 25 2021 to September 10 2021.
  • Interview panel will review the entries in the third phase, from September 15 2021 to 25 September 2021.
  • Awards will be declared in the first week of October 2021.

When and Where to Happen?

KITES Foundation will conduct the Award Ceremony, on 15 October 2021, tentatively in Thiruvananthapuram, in connection with celebrating its 2 years in the service sector and also commemorating the Birth Anniversary of Dr. APJ ABDUL KALAM.
