ABOUT THE AWARDS ABOUT

Award for the Best Volunteer

The Best Volunteer Award is presented to recognize exemplary voluntary contributions by young individuals in the service sector. It aims to encourage youth to engage in meaningful voluntary activities. This award reflects the exceptional array of individuals working in our nation's vibrant charity and non-profit sectors.

Eligibility Criteria for the Best Volunteer Award
  • The volunteer must be an Indian citizen.
  • The volunteer should be at least 11 years old and not more than 30 years.
  • Minimum of six months of volunteering experience, by either working or running an NGO, or contributing to society positively in your unique ways.
Award for the Best NGO/Community

The Best NGO/Community Award is presented to the outstanding functionaries to recognize their unwavering voluntary contributions to society. The award is to celebrate the non-government non-profit organizations or communities’ efforts and reinforce their practices in our society. The award also encourages greater participation from the young individuals to work in their community and beyond, for the nation's development.

Eligibility Criteria for the Best NGO/Community Award
  • NGO must be registered in India or headed by an Indian citizen.
  • Founded or/and currently running with Indian citizens below the age of 40 as Board members
  • If an NGO, it must be registered under any of these:
    • Society Registration Act
    • Company Registration Act
    • Nehru Youth Kendra Sangathan (NYKS)
  • The activities of the last two years will be mainly considered for the award.
  • Organizations or the community can be focused on youth-led developments achieving sustainable development goals, providing aid for the well-being of under-privileged communities, and building support mechanisms for their upliftment.
Award for the Best Project

The Best Project Award hunts impactful projects that inspire a whole new way of bringing about a ‘change’. The award will recognize those wonderful projects that were rooted in an honest purpose to bring about sustainable development.

Eligibility Criteria for the Best Project Award
  • An initiative by an NGO registered in India or headed by an Indian citizen.
  • The project must be at least 60% completed.
  • The beneficiary of the project must be any Indian community, as a whole or a section of it, rather than a specific individual.
  • The project should meet a minimum of three Sustainable Development Goals (SDG).
  • Uniqueness and innovation of the idea, primary purpose and immediate beneficiaries of the project, scale of success in terms of development in the community, etc. will be evaluated.

Application Process

  • Applications can be submitted through the online portal available on the website.
  • Applicants can submit the entries by themselves. Nominations and recommendations by a second party are also accepted. In case of project awards, an NGO can submit a maximum of 3 projects.
  • Selected entries for the second phase need to submit a detailed report of their activity, in the prescribed format. In the cases where nominated or recommended entries get selected to the second phase, the nominee is solely responsible to proceed with, and not the applicant. In no case, KITES Foundation will be responsible if the nominee refuses to proceed further, and that entry will stand cancelled thereafter.
  • Entries selected for the third and final phase need to report before an interview panel in person (for volunteer award), with all or minimum 3 board members (for NGO/community award), with all or minimum 3 project coordinators (for project award).

Important Dates

  • Applications will be open from 5th August 2021 to 20th August 2021.
  • Detailed report submission of the selected candidates will be from 25th August 2021 to 10th September 2021.
  • The interview panel will review the entries in the third phase, from 15th September 2021 to 25th September 2021.
  • Awards will be declared in the first week of October 2021.

The Award Ceremony

The Award Ceremony will be conducted on 15th October 2021, tentatively at Thiruvananthapuram, commemorating the Birth Anniversary of Dr. APJ ABDUL KALAM and celebrating the second anniversary of KITES Foundation.
