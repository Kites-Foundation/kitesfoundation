KITES Foundation

KITES Foundation is a not-for-profit, non-governmental organization that strives to channelise the power of productive youth engagement on various fronts to facilitate a ‘Youth-Led Development’ through multiple sectors including Education, Environment, Livelihood, Arts and Culture, Media, Technology etc.
