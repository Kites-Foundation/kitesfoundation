    What ?

    The Future Project is a lab for social innovation where we tinker with your innovative ideas! Pitch your theory of change and we’ll help you to make them a reality. We’ll work together with you to bring your ideas that could create grassroots-level solutions for social issues.

    Know More ->

    This is a very unique opportunity for you to make an impact, learn, network, and improve your social skills.The Future Project enables you to access the environment, the skills, the materials and the mentorship to make the best out of your idea. Our mission is not just about ideas that matter, but also about creating opportunities, especially in the development sector.

    Why ?

    Through The Future Project, we want to build a movement of youth-led initiatives to nurture future leaders and changemakers by providing the needed resources and support to put their ideas into action.

    Know More ->

    Since the project is about giving, sharing, supporting causes you care about, and creating a better future for everyone, we would like to call it ‘The Future Project’. As the powerhouse of the nation, we wanted you, the young people, to imagine and work for a better future for all of us.

    How ?

    Young minds can pitch you theories of change/ideas and we will provide you with the resources that you need to take it to the next level.

    Know More ->

    Our model is fundamentally based on four main processes - recruitment, training & mentorship, execution and rewards to build purpose-driven Future Projects that can impact the immediate communities around the changemakers while creating a culture of mutual support, inspiration, and empowerment.




    The Future Project helps you to:
    • Take charge of your ideas
    • Innovate in your ways
    • build a group of doers to achieve your bigger social goals.


    We offer you :
    • capacity-building activities
    • one-to-one mentorship
    • peer-review programs
    • funding assistance
    • knowledge sessions
    • partnerships and collaborations to propagate your ideas.
    • access to networking platforms and communities of practice.
