Frequently Asked Questions

KITES Foundation is a not-for-profit, non- governmental organization registered under Societies Registration Act, 1860. For an organisation to be termed as charity it requires Income tax clearances under Clause 12 A of Income Tax Act so KITES is technically a charity. Though our projects are fundamentally service oriented they are dynamic and seek to explore a new dimension of charity.

  • To incubate and celebrate transformative individual ideas through collective effort to build leaders and social entrepreneurs, capable of responding to the new challenges.
  • To identify and resolve community-based problems with desirable solutions by joining hands with the Government machinery.
  • To engage in quality knowledge generation through research to facilitate informed actions.
  • To explore the potential of youth networking within and outside the nation
Organizational Goal

To build a unique functional model of the organization that can serve as a guiding light for generations to come and create scalable social impact in national and international levels.

Community Goal

To channelize the power of productive youth engagements in various fronts to facilitate a ‘Youth-Led’ development.

  • Environment - Green Troopers
