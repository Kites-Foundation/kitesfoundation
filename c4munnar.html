    About Us

    Code4Munnar is a 3-day coding camp happening at the beautiful hill station of Munnar. Kites Foundation, along with the district administration of Idukki, is conducting the event to boost the tourism sector of Munnar. The brightest minds and tech geeks are coming together along with visionaries to reshape the face of Munnar tourism with the power of technology. Let's come, code and have fun together. Together, let's make the tourism at Munnar stronger and beautiful than ever.
