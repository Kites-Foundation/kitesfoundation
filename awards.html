our Initiatives

TREK FOR A CAUSE!

The urge of youngsters to travel and explore the world can be utilized for a social cause.

  • Trek for a Cause is an initiative by Kites to promote social volunteering through traveling.
  • It involves a volunteer call, registration, trekking along with solving a cause.

A youth collective is using its passion for traveling to create a social impact, bring change, and ensure responsible tourism through Trek for a Cause.

Tree of life - there is hope!

KITES India joins hands with Aster DM Healthcare and DQF to launch Tree of Life, a noble initiative for children from underprivileged sections of our society

  • The project will assure quality healthcare, accessible to children by providing life-saving surgeries and necessary interventional procedures free of cost across Kerala.
  • To help their families from the vicious cycle of poverty that could arise from mounting medical costs.

Tree of Life caters to Pediatrics Surgeries exclusively for underprivileged children. The initiative secures the beneficiaries advanced treatments and post-operative care.

BOOKFARM!

We intend to rebuild and set up well-functioning libraries across Kerala by collaborating with the Library Council, various NGOs, and reading clubs in the stat

  • Bookfarm is an initiative to rescue libraries that are struggling for proper funds and maintenance and to build new ones for reviving the passion of reading among all sections.
  • It is to tackle the divide in the accessibility of libraries and learning opportunities between the privileged and weaker sections.

Book Farm plans to rebuild and set up well-functioning libraries in every district of Kerala in two years.

Munnar VIBGYOR Tourism

Kites Foundation in association with District Administration, Idukki has initiated a Tourism development project in Munnar.

  • The tourism project was launched by Shri.M.M Mani and Shri.C Ravindranath Honorable Ministers of Power and Education respectively.
  • Hello Munnar, a progressive web app designed for the ease of information access was also launched in the ceremony.

The project seeks to improve the discoverability of Munnar by providing necessary route based informations to the tourists. For an enhanced tourism experience, Seven routes have been identified in Munnar which are aptly described by the seven rainbow colours, The VIBGYOR.
