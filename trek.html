About Us

Trek for a cause is an initiative driven by the urge of youngsters to travel and explore the world. The aim of Trek for a cause is to use this urge and enthusiasm for a social cause judiciously. The social cause varies according to places and situations. Ultimately a youth collective is using its passion for traveling to create a social impact and bring change through Trek for a Cause.

Action Plan


  • Step 1
    Selection of destination along with an appropriate cause that needs volunteer support.
  • Step 2
    Call for volunteers, their registration and payment.
  • Step 3
    Trekking to the destination with the team.

Our Vision

The aim of Trek for a Cause is to provide a cognizance of how to travel and remain environmentally and socially conscious at the same time.

Our Mission

To create opportunities for people who are unable to explore the world along with involving them to do a small bit to bring change in our society.

Our Plan

To familiarize the environment, and constitute social impact through volunteering by organizing Trek for a Cause once or twice a month in every district of Kerala and across India.
