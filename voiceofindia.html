    The Event

    Youngsters accross the country will speak on their ‘My vision of a future India’, in their own regional language.

    When and Where?

    On our official Youtube channel and other social media platforms on August 15th, 2020.

    What?

    Our aim is to discuss and educate ourselves on the ideas, thoughts and concepts to make the world’s largest democracy great and powerful.

    About Us

    "An imagined political community and imagined as both inherently limited and sovereign. It is imagined because the members of even the smallest nation will never know most of their fellow-members, meet them, or even hear of them, yet in the minds of each lives the image of their communion"
    -Benedict Anderson-

    When Benedict Anderson coins the idea of Nation as an imagined community, it's more of a sad reality especially in hard times like this. It's high time, we, the youth of this nation, come, speak and act together to fulfil, what Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam dreamt of transforming India into a New Millennium of Power, Energy, Vision and Mission, thus making the nation a superpower in the world

    Our Vision

    Kites Foundation is a team of young social change makers who aim at transforming this country, piece by piece and soul by soul through youth led development and collective effort. Here, we are to break the chains of linguistic-political-religious-geographical barriers and to dream for the idea of a superpower India.

    Our Mission

    Youth represents the most vibrant and potentially rich community of any nation. If this community is properly channelized, it has the power to bring in great change to the nation as a whole.74 years have passed by since Independence, we believe it's high time,the youth, the greater collective of this country come together to see the power or organisation in changing the society.

    Our Plan

    On August 15 as part of the 74th Independence Day celebrations, Kites Foundation is opening up the virtual spaces of democracy for a public speaking event *The Voice of India*.As we are looking forward to a post-pandemic future, youngsters from across the country will speak their views on 'My Vision of a Developed India' in their own regional Languages.

    Format

    Speak Out and Let Your Voice Be Heard! Here are the directives.

    Register

    Register through the link provided in our official Website. Participant Registration will be closed on 1st of August, 2020 at 12 AM. You will get a Confirmation letter after the registration process.

    Submission

    Submission of prerecorded videos (maximum length of the video must be 5 minutes)to the official Whatsapp account/Mail ID with a self attested copy of hand written Declaration as mentioned.

    Streaming

    After the process of verification and approval by a panel including representatives from each state, selected videos will be streamed online through our official Youtube channel and other social media platforms on August 15th, 2020.
