    What?

    "Voice of India" is our virtual speaking platform for the passionate youth to share innovative ideas and concepts to build a better future.

    The Event

    Youngsters from across the nation will speak on their idea of ‘My Vision of India’ in their own regional languages.

    When and Where?

    On our official Youtube channel and other social media platforms on Aug 15, 2021.

    About Us

    “ Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action. ”
    -APJ Abdul Kalam

    Voice of India is our national level speaking event conducted to share and express groundbreaking ideas and concepts for a moral purpose. This Independence day, while our nation is still battling the pandemic, let us come together to show our unity and dedication towards our country. Anyone below the age of 40 can speak about ‘My Vision of a future India’ and contribute their vision of a more powerful India in their regional languages.
    The participants are required to submit their videos of no more than 5 minutes to our official WhatsApp number- +91 8138000935 or email- voiceofindia@kitesfoundation.org, with a self-attested copy of the handwritten declaration within 5 days after the registration process as specified in the confirmation letter.
    Let us remind ourselves of our vast culture and multiplicity by coming together with our diversified ideas for a more unified and powerful India.

    Our Vision

    Youth-led, unified, India.

    Our Mission

    To provide a platform for the Indian youth to speak for the betterment of our country.

    Our Plan

    Kites coordinators will assess the videos submitted on and before August 1 and on the 74th Independence Day, we will live-stream the selected submissions on ‘My Vision of a future India’ on our official social media handles. All the participants will receive certificates and the opportunity to share their vision with a large audience.

    Format

    Speak Out and Let Your Voice Be Heard! Here are the directives.

    Register

    Register for the event through our official website on or before August 1, 2021. A confirmation letter will be sent after the registration process.

    Submission

    Participants have to submit pre-recorded videos of no more than 5 minutes to our official Whatsapp or email (mentioned above) with a self-attested copy of the handwritten declaration.

    Streaming

    After the process of verification and approval by a panel including representatives from each state, selected videos will be streamed online through our official Youtube channel and other social media platforms on August 15th, 2021.
