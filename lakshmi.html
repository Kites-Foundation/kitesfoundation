Kites defines this sobering fact. Proximity to the people is the vital strength of every community based organization.Kites ensuring this principle overtly through the change making projects.

'My Santa', the project of sharing immense happiness and smile. The moment of giving and taking of love among human beings.
Social commitment and connectivity is phenomenal to realize the wider social goals. Kites put emphasis on change makers and deliver desirable reforms in the society. Being a volunteer of Kites makes me fathomable the true meaning of volunteering that directs to make positive social impacts in the society.As a young social visionary , Kites marked it's stupendous place in my career of Volunteering.
