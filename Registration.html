    Our Vision

    Kites Foundation is a team of young social change makers who aim at transforming this country, piece by piece and soul by soul through youth led development and collective effort. Here, we are to break the chains of linguistic-political-religious-geographical barriers and to dream for the idea of a superpower India.

    Our Mission

    Youth represents the most vibrant and potentially rich community of any nation. If this community is properly channelized, it has the power to bring in great change to the nation as a whole.74 years have passed by since Independence, we believe it's high time,the youth, the greater collective of this country come together to see the power or organisation in changing the society.
