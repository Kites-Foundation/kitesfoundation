Kites Internship Programme

Are you ready to step out? It’s your turn to lead!!

Apply Now

Who can apply?

Anyone available for a duration of 3 months; from Nov 10, 2021 to March 11, 2022.
Have relevant skills and interests

How to Apply?

Step 1- Fill the given google form.
Step 2- Shortlisted interns will be contacted via email for online interaction with the Kites officials.

About

Kites Foundation is excited to invite young, aspirant minds to join a noble venture through a unique internship experience. As a youth-led organisation, dedicated to collective action, Kites is extending a dual opportunity to learn and contribute to societal development. This internship is a great opportunity for those who are passionate about improving their skills and channelising them for noble causes.

The interns have the freedom to choose among the 6 areas of their choice; education, media, environment, health, livelihood and technology, for better development in their skill and experience.

  • 2. Graphic designer
  • 3. Photographer
  • 4. Editor

1. Content Writing (work from home)

Content writers under Kites are passionate creators, dedicated to influencing positive change in society. Writing for Kites is not limited to improving skill and experience; it extends to channelling art towards spreading the organisation's message of collective action for a better tomorrow and by doing so, being part of the change that everyone wants to see in this world.

Selected interns are expected to support the causes that Kites stand for and effectively contribute to the team's creative output.

Responsibilities:

    Create original website, technical and social media content, blog posts, campaign posts, emails and copy written content.

    Edit and proofread content.

    Engage in content brainstorming with the media team, other interns and volunteers.

    Employ SEO (search engine optimization) strategies to devise content tailored for the website, social media and blog posts.

    Write and edit content within the given time frame

Preferred Skills

    Spoken and written proficiency in either one or more languages mentioned- English, Malayalam, Tamil and Hindi.

    Creative and technical writing skills.

    Able to deliver content within the specified time period.

    Excellent communication skills; both verbal and written. verbal and written.

    Dedicated towards social work and willingness to work together.

    Proficiency in MS Word and Google docs.

    Good research skills

    Adaptable to manage different types of content.

What we offer:

    Certificate and experience letter

    Skill development.

    Professional experience.

    Flexible work hours.

Best performing interns will be selected into the Media team for more exciting projects!

What makes this internship unique?

Rather than assigning work from day one, Kites is offering a proper orientation and guidance for the interns so that they understand the concept of social work, the organisation of Kites, its structure and its team better. Along with that, certified workshops for technical internships like web development and graphic designing by experts will also be provided. The interns will be able to work in a friendly environment to grow their skills and connect with the Kites family.
