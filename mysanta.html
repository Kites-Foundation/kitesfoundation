    Hand of Happiness

    As we look forward to uplifting the lives of many after the pandemic, we have initiated it from the most unprivileged kids who share the whole part of their life on wheelchairs.Our volunteers across the nation have joined hands to personally visit each of them at their home and put a smile on their little faces !

    Cake for a sake

    As part of the My Santa project, we had initiated a cake donation programme through home bakers who showed their deep intention of giving. The cake reached the unprivileged as a gift wrapped in happiness and kindness. The volunteers also made sure that the right flavours reached the right flavour lovers.

    Gift with Love

    Gifting is a significant part of expressing humanitarian values. While we never fail to receive the most favorite gifts for many of our occasions, we saw those little faces who might not have that opportunity.We deliberately found their most desiring gifts, and wrapped it with our love . As we believe that gifting is also about spending some quality time with our loved ones, our volunteers made it efficient by personally reaching out to each of them.

    Why?

    Christmas is the most wonderful time of the year for many of us, but not for all. For many amongst us, this year it has turned out to be the most depressing or stressful due to the pandemic, especially for children. This is where "My Santa" has risen above all, bringing cheer and gifts to whoever may not get one likely. We provide them not just a gift box, but a special Christmas which they would remember.

    When and Where?

    My Santa project was run during the Christmas season, in order to ensure that the differently-abled children also find their true joy as others do. Also, Christmas being a season of love, it's a self-development in the volunteers to have a direct approach with the children to realise how abled they are and how compassionate they should be to others. My Santa had a flying start at MGLC Anandamkudi School near Kuttampuzha.The next phase of My Santa will begin by December 2021.

    Who?

    Through My Santa, we typically target people on wheelchairs who experience a complete lockdown throughout their life. Apart from this we also found disabled children, orphans or financially less fortunate children to be well deserving to be part of this initiative. Rather than reaching out to orphanages, schools or old age homes who usually get to receive a lot of privileges, we targeted the people who are isolated at home due to their physical or mental disabilities.

    About Us

    My Santa is a project initiated by the Kites Foundation to brighten the tiny lives darkened by the shadow of the pandemic. The project aims at sharing happiness with the disabled who are orphans, wheelchair users and senior citizens. The process consisted of donating wheelchairs to the needy and a package including a cake and their favorite present as a gift hamper. What makes it unique is that we have found the most needful people, and figured out their most desirable gift from their guardians, and surprised them with the same wrapped in bunch of love and happiness, along with their favorite cake.My Santa was held splendidly across all districts of Kerala, recalling the importance of cultivating the value of acceptance and appreciation of everyone in our society.

    Sharing is caring. Giving is loving. With immense happiness, we are proud to proclaim that the My Santa initiative by the Kites Foundation bloomed smiles on many faces. My Santa made this Christmas special for all of us. We would like to extend our words of gratitude to all Santa’s who joined us in the journey. Such little acts of kindness by you all made 2020 a bright one despite the darkness. We hope you all would join us in our future endeavours as well.

    Our Vision

    My Santa is not just a charity project for the Kites family. Rather, it is something special that made a bonding amongst volunteers and the children. Even after the Christmas season, we tried to keep in touch with those children. Our Vision strives in ensuring the comfortability of each of our beneficiaries by being their own companions and there by making them mentally peaceful and strong.

    Our Mission

    Kites Foundation is a team of young social change makers who aim at transforming this country, through youth led development and collective effort. Through My Santa, we aimed at utilizing our togetherness to spread happiness among children and their family by our young volunteers themself as santas. We try to bring out their fantasy stories of Santa Claus visiting them with their favorite christmas gift to real life.

    Our Plan

    My Santa will be initiated every year to spread the smiles through our enchanting volunteers across the nation. The plan goes parallely by finding the most deserving beneficiaries from each district, and understanding their likes and dislikes to choose a gift for them.The next step initiates in welcoming cake donations from compassionate home bakers and preparing the favorite flavored cake for each beneficiary.
