About The Awards

“The unselfish effort to bring cheer to others will be the beginning of a happier life for ourselves.”
- Helen Keller

Volunteerism is an important and increasingly popular system for young people to devote their time, effort, and talent to a need, cause, or mission without financial gain. As we realize that volunteerism has gained very minimal recognition in our country, it is our core responsibility to recognize and appreciate the Volunteers, and Volunteer activities across the nation.

Kites Foundation announces KITES Awards 2021 to acknowledge and appreciate the journey of young individuals and communities who have volunteered and contributed positively to bring about a constructive change in our society and led a step forward to ensure a better state of living from the developmental aspect. Through this initiative, we aim to find individuals who are highly passionate about contributing to activities that lead to a social impact and development of unprivileged communities. In a world that seeks change, we create the ‘change-makers’!

Category of Awards

Award for the Best Volunteer

The Best Volunteer Award is presented to recognize exemplary voluntary contributions by young individuals in the service sector. It aims to encourage youth to engage in meaningful voluntary activities. This award reflects the exceptional array of individuals working in our nation's vibrant charity and non-profit sectors.

Award for the Best NGO/Community

The Best NGO/Community Award is presented to the outstanding functionaries to recognize their unwavering voluntary contributions to society. The award is to celebrate the non-government non-profit organizations or communities’ efforts and reinforce their practices in our society. The award also encourages greater participation from the young individuals to work in their community and beyond, for the nation's development.

Award for the Best Project

The Best Project Award hunts impactful projects that inspire a whole new way of bringing about a ‘change’. The award will recognize those wonderful projects that were rooted in an honest purpose to bring about sustainable development.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Kites Awards 2021?

    This is an initiative by Kites Foundation to recognize and appreciate the passionate volunteers of our country, who have contributed their efforts, talent, and time to the betterment of our nation. We have the largest youth population in the world, yet the act of volunteerism has very minimal recognition in our country. So these awards aim to promote the volunteering activities in our society.

  • How can I be a volunteer?

    There are millions of young individuals and communities who contribute positively to bring about a constructive change in our society and takes necessary steps to ensure a better state of living from the developmental aspect.
    If you have volunteered in any of these categories, then you are a volunteer

    • Institutions/Colleges (NSS,NYKS,CSOs)
    • NGOs
    • Community (Working directly with a certain community.)
    • Social entrepreneurs

  • What are the different categories of awards?

    There are 3 types of Kites Awards 2021

    • Award for the Best Volunteer
      This award aims in encouraging passionate young volunteers who have contributed to the service sector, without any financial gain. It aims to encourage youth to engage in meaningful voluntary activities.
    • Award for the Best NGO/Community
      The award is to celebrate the non-government non-profit organizations or communities’ efforts and reinforce their practices in our society. The award also encourages greater participation from the young individuals to work in their community and beyond, for the nation's development.
    • Award for the Best Project
      The Best Project Award hunts impactful projects that inspire a whole new way of bringing about a ‘change’. The award will recognize those wonderful projects that were rooted in an honest purpose to bring about sustainable development.

  • When can we start applying and when is the deadline?

    Applications will be open from 5th August 2021 to 20th August 2021. Detailed report submission of the selected candidates will be from 25th August 2021 to 10th September 2021. The interview panel will review the entries in the third phase, from 15th September 2021 to 25th September 2021.

  • What are the Eligibility Criteria for applying to the ‘ Best Volunteer Award‘?

    • The volunteer must be an Indian citizen.
    • The volunteer should be at least 11 years old and not more than 30 years.
    • Minimum of six months of volunteering experience, by either working or running an NGO, or contributing to society positively in your unique ways.

  • What are the Eligibility Criteria for the Best NGO Award and Best Project Award?

    Eligibility Criteria for the Best NGO/Community Award
    • NGO must be registered in India or headed by an Indian citizen.
    • Founded or/and currently running with Indian citizens below the age of 40 as Board members
    • If an NGO, it must be registered under any of these:
      • Society Registration Act
      • Company Registration Act
      • Nehru Youth Kendra Sangathan (NYKS)
    • The activities of the last two years will be mainly considered for the award.
    • Organizations or the community can be focused on youth-led developments achieving sustainable development goals, providing aid for the well-being of under-privileged communities, and building support mechanisms for their upliftment.

    Eligibility Criteria for the Best Project Award
    • An initiative by an NGO registered in India or headed by an Indian citizen.
    • The project must be at least 60% completed.
    • The beneficiary of the project must be any Indian community, as a whole or a section of it, rather than a specific individual.
    • The project should meet a minimum of three Sustainable Development Goals (SDG).
    • Uniqueness and innovation of the idea, primary purpose and immediate beneficiaries of the project, scale of success in terms of development in the community, etc. will be evaluated.

  • When will the results be announced?

    Awards will be declared in the first week of October 2021 and the Award Ceremony will be conducted on 15th October 2021, tentatively at Thiruvananthapuram, commemorating the Birth Anniversary of Dr. APJ ABDUL KALAM and celebrating the second anniversary of KITES Foundation.

  • How can I apply?

    • Applications can be submitted through the online portal available on the website.
    • Applicants can submit the entries by themselves. Nominations and recommendations by a second party are also accepted. In case of project awards, an NGO can submit a maximum of 3 projects

  • What are the procedures after getting shortlisted?

    • Selected entries for the second phase need to submit a detailed report of their activity, in the prescribed format. In the cases where nominated or recommended entries get selected to the second phase, the nominee is solely responsible to proceed with, and not the applicant. In no case, KITES Foundation will be responsible if the nominee refuses to proceed further, and that entry will stand cancelled thereafter.
    • Entries selected for the third and final phase need to report before an interview panel in person (for volunteer award), with all or minimum 3 board members (for NGO/community award), with all or minimum 3 project coordinators (for project award).

  • How will the awards benefit me?

    Kites Awards is a remarkable opportunity for passionate individuals in the service sector to showcase their works and talents in front of society. The award recognizes their selfless efforts that served the society to ensure a better development of the nation.

  • .I have contributed through online volunteering, or work from home, can I still apply?

    Yes, both online, as well as offline volunteering, will be considered.

  • How can I ask more queries related to Kites Awards 2021?

    You can reach out to us by directly contacting the official numbers mentioned on the website, or email us at info@kitesfoundation.org.

  • How can I promote Kites Awards 2021?

    As a Volunteer, Organisation, Institute/College, NGO, Community, or Social Entrepreneur, you can promote Kites Awards 2021 through your social media network by using the website link, or posters that are officially released by Kites Foundation. Encouraging the volunteers and organizations in your social circles to nominate themselves for the award will be highly appreciated. Please make sure not to make any changes to the existing posters and other collaterals.

  • What are the Evaluation Criteria of the awards?

    The major Evaluation criteria of deciding the winners will be,

    • 1. Impact
    • 2. Engagement with Community
    • 3. Evidence
    • 4. Reference
    However, the final decisions will be taken by the expert Jury Panel of ‘Kites Awards 2021’.

    Can I edit my response?

    No, unfortunately, you won’t be able to edit your response after submitting it, so please make sure that you fill up the form very clearly. Also, please keep a separate copy of all your answers submitted through the application form
